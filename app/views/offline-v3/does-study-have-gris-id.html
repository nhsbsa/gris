What is a GRIS ID?

A GRIS ID is a unique code for each new study, including sub-studies.

It helps to track how the study develops and keeps all of the study information connected.

Anyone involved in the study can create a GRIS ID for example, a sponsor or a researcher.

It's the responsibility of the chief investigator to share the GRIS ID with all research study team members.
