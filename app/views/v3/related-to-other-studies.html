A study is related to another study if:

  • it is a sub-study of another study (this applies to studies where patients that participate in a parent study take part in additional optional activity to that of the parent study)
  • it is a parent study of another study (this applies to standalone studies that may have related studies)
  • it is a platform study (this applies to studies with a master protocol that is made up of addendums and study arms that may have sub-protocols)
  • it is a continuation study (this applies to studies that typically recruit participations from the parent study but have a different IRAS ID to that of the parent study)
