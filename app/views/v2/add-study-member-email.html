Do you need to add additional study team members?

You must add at least one additional study team member. You do not need to add all study team members, only those who will potentially require access to the update information stored about the study. More study members can be added at a later stage.

Study team members will automatically be given read-only access. If a study team member needs to be able to edit the information stored about a study, you can give them edit-access by ticking the check box below.
