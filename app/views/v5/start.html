Sign in to your GOV.UK One Login

Use this service to:

  • create a unique government research identifier service ID (GRIS ID)
  • view an existing study
  • make changes to an existing study

Before you start

You'll need a GOV.UK One Login to use this service.

If you have not used GOV.UK One Login to access this service before, sign in using your existing sign in details. You'll then be asked to create or sign in to GOV.UK One Login.

In future, you should then always use your GOV.UK One Login to sign in to this service.

What you'll need to register a new study

To create a GRIS ID for your new study, you'll need details, such as:

  • the short title of the study
  • the disease area or speciality
  • if it has a government strategic priority
  • study status, phase, setting, and location
  • chief investigator's details
  • team member details
  • the sponsoring organisation's details

It would be helpful to know the details of any related study, such as the GRIS ID, IRAS ID, and funding ID.
